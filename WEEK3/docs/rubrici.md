# Assessment rubric — Week 3 (lab)

## Excellent (9–10)
- Correctly explains UDP broadcast, UDP multicast and TCP forwarding
- Demonstrates reproducible runs with clear commands and outputs
- Provides a packet capture excerpt with correct interpretation of headers
- Discusses at least two pitfalls and how they were mitigated

## Good (7–8)
- Correct implementation and runs, minor gaps in interpretation
- Artefacts are present and the smoke test passes
- Packet capture included but with limited commentary

## Satisfactory (5–6)
- Runs succeed but explanation is superficial
- Packet capture missing or not interpretable
- Some outputs are incomplete or not reproducible

## Insufficient (<5)
- Demos do not run reliably
- No evidence of verification or traffic inspection
