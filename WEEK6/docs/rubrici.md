# Assessment rubric (Week 6)

Your work is assessed on reproducibility, correctness and clarity.

## Technical execution

- Demo runs non-interactively and produces the required artefacts
- Clean-up resets the environment without manual intervention
- Smoke test passes

## Technical understanding

- Correct explanations of NAT and PAT
- Correct interpretation of at least one ARP exchange
- Correct interpretation of at least one control-plane behaviour in SDN

## Communication

- Commands are shown as code blocks
- Observations are linked to evidence (logs or captures)
- Writing is concise and technically accurate
